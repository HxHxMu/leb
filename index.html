About

Luis E. Bonilla is a Product Designer / UX–UI Lead working across consumer applications, platforms, and product systems. His practice emphasizes information architecture, design system alignment, prototyping, and iterative refinement within collaborative environments. Work often occurs inside established products where contribution patterns matter more than isolated artifacts.

In parallel, he produces music through HxHxM (Haux Haux Music)—a catalog architecture encompassing legacy bands, collaborative projects, and fictional artist identities. This work centers on metadata integrity, credit continuity, and long-term authorship clarity across distribution platforms. This page connects both identities in a single verified reference node.
